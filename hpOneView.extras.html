hpOneView.extras.mergers.merge_list_by_key(original_list, updated_list, key, ignore_when_null=[])[source]¶

Merge two lists by the key. It basically: 1. Adds the items that are present on updated_list and are absent on original_list. 2. Removes items that are absent on updated_list and are present on original_list. 3. For all items that are in both lists, overwrites the values from the original item by the updated item.

Parameters:
  • original_list – original list.
  • updated_list – list with changes.
  • key – unique identifier.
  • ignore_when_null – list with the keys from the updated items that should be ignored in the merge, if its
  • are null. (values) –
Returns:

Lists merged.
Return type:

list
